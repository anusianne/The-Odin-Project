Recipe of Greek Salad

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can black olives
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup feta cheese
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ground black pepper to taste
