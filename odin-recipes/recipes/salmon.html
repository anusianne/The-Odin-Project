Description

Moist and tender salmon with a slightly sweet and salty crust—it's the perfect bite! This recipe is easy and a nice choice to serve for guests who aren't used to eating salmon. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh parsley and serve alongside rice pilaf or roasted sweet potatoes.

Recipe of Salmon

    Ingredients:
  • olive oil spray
  • 2 tablespoons butted
  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 tablespoon Greek seasoning
  • 1 medium lemon
    Steps to be prepared:
  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil and spray with nonstick spray.
  2. Lay salmon, skin-side down, on the prepared baking sheet. Spread butter mixture evenly over the salmon. Sprinkle with Greek seasoning and drizzle with ½ of the lemon juice.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, 17 to 20 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with remaining lemon juice and serve.
